Welcome to ICCSAT 2025

The 1st International Conference on Computing and Sustainable Automation Technology is a global platform for researchers, experts, and practitioners to explore the intersection of computing, automation, and sustainability.

It focuses on leveraging AI, Machine Learning, IoT, and Robotics to address environmental challenges through energy efficiency, resource optimization, and sustainable practices.

The conference fosters collaboration, networking, and the exchange of cutting-edge research to shape a more sustainable technological future.
